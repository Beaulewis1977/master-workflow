# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community.

- Be respectful and considerate. Harassment and discrimination are not tolerated.
- Provide constructive feedback; focus on ideas, not people.
- Use inclusive language; assume good intent; resolve conflicts in good faith.
- Report unacceptable behavior via private channels referenced in `SECURITY.md`.

Violations may result in warnings or removal from the community at maintainers' discretion.
